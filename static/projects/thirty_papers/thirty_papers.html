30 Papers in 30 Days

Summary

To prepare for a potential Master's or PhD in Artificial Intelligence, I need to build a strong foundational knowledge of the current research landscape. While I have an extensive background in software engineering, I lack formal research experience. This project is a self-imposed challenge to bridge that gap by reading and summarizing one AI research paper every day for 30 days.

My notes will be shared below. They probably won't be updated same-day, so depending on when you look at this it could appear a bit behind, but I'll do my best to get them all up here shortly after the end of this 30 day experiment on July 30th, 2025. They also may be a bit informal since I'm primarily doing this for myself; providing value to you through this blog is only a secondary goal.
