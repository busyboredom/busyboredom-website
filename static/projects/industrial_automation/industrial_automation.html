Industrial Automation

Summary

I went into industrial automation out of college, so this is a bit more than one project. It's how I paid my loans off, and oh boy does it take a lot of programming to pay off a degree nowadays.

At Universal Machine and Engineering, the role of a controls engineer is to program machines to do the tasks expected of them. Those tasks range from putting bottles in boxes to cutting up lobsters -- the client specifies the task, the mechanical guys build a machine, and the controls guys bring it to life.
